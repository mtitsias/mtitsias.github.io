About

Michalis Titsias is full time Research Scientist at DeepMind based on London, UK. Before he was an Assistant Professor in the department of Informatics at Athens University of Economics and Business (AUEB), and Postdoctoral Researcher at the University of Oxford and University of Manchester. He obtained his Ph.D from University of Edinburgh. He holds an undergraduate Diploma and M.Sc. degree in Computer Science from University of Ioannina, Greece.

Research Interests

  • Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Reinforcement Learning
  • Continual Learning, Transfer Learning
  • Gaussian Processes, Bayesian Methods
  • MCMC, Variational Inference
  • Stochastic Gradient Estimation
