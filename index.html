About

I work as is full time Research Scientist at Google DeepMind in London, UK. Before I was an Assistant Professor in the department of Informatics at Athens University of Economics and Business (AUEB), and Postdoctoral Researcher at the University of Oxford and University of Manchester. I obtained my PhD from University of Edinburgh and previously I studied Computer Science in Greece at the University of Ioannina.

Research Interests

  • Machine Learning, Statistical Methodology
  • Continual Learning, Transfer Learning
  • Gaussian Processes, Bayesian Methods
  • MCMC, Variational Inference, Stochastic Gradient Estimation
  • Diffusion Models
